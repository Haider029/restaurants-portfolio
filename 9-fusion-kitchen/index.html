Our Philosophy

No borders,
no limits. Just great food.

Chef Kamran Malik trained in Tokyo, Paris, and New York — then came home to Lahore with a head full of ideas and a deep love for Pakistani spices.

The result? A menu that lets a Pakistani karahi share a table with a Japanese yuzu glaze, where saffron meets truffle and it somehow just works.
