Our Story

Made with Love,
Served with Pride

Chai Dhaba started as a small charpoy under a neem tree on Canal Road. Today we have three locations across Lahore — but the recipes haven't changed a day.

Every morning we grind our own masalas. Every pot is slow-cooked on wood fire. Because great food can't be rushed.
